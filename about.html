About Public Health Tracker

Public Health Tracker is a community health reporting web application aimed at connecting citizens and healthcare providers. It allows users to submit health reports from their local areas, enabling early detection and response to health-related issues.

The platform is designed to be simple and accessible to everyone, ensuring that health incidents can be easily reported and managed. Through this initiative, we aim to strengthen community involvement in public health surveillance.
